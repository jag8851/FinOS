Welcome to Coastline Finance

Your finances, your data.

Coastline Finance lives entirely in your browser. All data goes straight to your own Google Sheet — no servers, no third parties, no ads.

  • 📊  Import Capital One CSV statements
  • 🏷️  Auto-categorize with a rules engine
  • 📈  Net Worth & spending dashboards
  • 🌊  Coast FIRE calculator & heat map

Connect Google

Sign in with Google to allow Coastline to read and write your Google Sheet. We only access files you explicitly authorize.

Connect your Google Sheet

Paste the Sheet ID from your Google Sheet's URL:
docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/SHEET_ID_HERE/edit

Creates Categories, Rules, Spending, Income tabs if they don't exist. Your Net Worth tab is untouched.

Set your PIN

Choose a 4-digit PIN to protect the app on your device. This is a UI lock — your real data is secured by Google.

Default PIN is 3083 if you skip this step.
